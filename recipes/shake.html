Protein Shake

Description

This recipe is a tutorial for a simple protein shake. Not much to say about it, It;s just a tasty protein shake. Tastes kind of like a milkshake but without ice cream.

Ingredients

  • One scoop of protein powder
  • 250ml of unsweetened almond milk
  • 100g of banana
  • 30g of natural peanut butter
  • You can also add some ice to thin the shake.

Steps

  1. The first thing you need to do is to get your blender setup.
  2. Next, measure out 250ml of unsweetened almond milk and pour in.
  3. For the third step, measure out the peanut butter, banana and protein powder. Pour into blender with the almond milk.
  4. Blend. And that is all. Now you have a protein shake.
