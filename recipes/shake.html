Protein Shake

Description

This recipe is a tutorial for a simple protein shake. Not much to say about it, It;s just a tasty protein shake. Tastes kind of like a milkshake but without ice cream.

Ingredients

  • One scoop of protein powder
  • 250ml of unsweetened almond milk
  • 100g of banana
  • 30g of natural peanut butter
  • You can also add some ice to thin the shake.
