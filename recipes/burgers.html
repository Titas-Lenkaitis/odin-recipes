My homemade burgers recipe

This page is a tutorial on how to make my burgers recipe. I am no expert cook so this recipe will be very simple. It's nothing fancy but I do find these burgers delicious and juicy. This recipe makes 2 burgers.

Ingredients

  • 4 burger buns
  • 2 patties
  • A red onion
  • A pickle
  • Some iceberg lettuce
  • Burger sauce
  • Sliced cheddar cheese or american cheese
  • Skin-on fries
